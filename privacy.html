Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

Intended Users

Scoopy Log is intended for use by parents or legal guardians. Children do not use the app directly.

What Data We Store

The data you enter in the app is stored only on your device and, if you enable iCloud sync, in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect or store this data on our servers. This includes:

  • Sleep tracking entries (times, durations)
  • Feeding entries (type, time, notes)
  • Diaper change entries (type, time)
  • Growth measurements (weight, height, date)
  • Child profile information (name, birth date, optional photo)

How Data Is Stored

On-Device Storage: Your data is stored on your device by default. We do not have access to data stored on your device.

Optional iCloud Sync: If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your data may be stored in your personal iCloud account. This is optional and controlled by you. When you use iCloud sync, your data is stored in your private iCloud account, which is managed by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Scoopy Log uses Apple's CloudKit framework for optional iCloud synchronization.

What We Do Not Do

Scoopy Log does not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use advertising or tracking technologies
  • Use third-party analytics or crash reporting services
  • Perform AI profiling or automated behavioral analysis
  • Collect data you do not explicitly enter

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS security features. If you use iCloud sync, your data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

Your Rights

You can delete your data at any time by deleting the app or using the app's data reset feature. If you use iCloud sync, you can disable it in your device settings.

Subscriptions and Payments

All subscription payments are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not receive or store payment information.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
