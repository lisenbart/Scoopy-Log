Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

Intended Users

Scoopy Log is intended for use by parents or legal guardians. Children do not use the app directly.

What Data We Store

The data you enter in the app is stored only on your device and, when you are signed into iCloud, in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect or store this data on our servers. This includes:

  • Sleep tracking entries (times, durations)
  • Feeding entries (type, time, notes)
  • Diaper change entries (type, time)
  • Growth measurements (weight, height, date)
  • Child profile information (name, birth date, optional photo)

How Data Is Stored

On-Device Storage: Your data is stored on your device by default. We do not have access to data stored on your device.

Optional iCloud Sync: When you are signed into iCloud on your device, Scoopy Log may store and sync your data (profiles, logs, and optional profile photos) in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data remains in your private iCloud account, which is managed by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data. You can stop syncing by signing out of iCloud in your device's system Settings, or by using the app's "Reset App Data" feature (which also clears data from iCloud for this app).

What We Do Not Do

Scoopy Log does not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use advertising or tracking technologies
  • Use third-party analytics or crash reporting services
  • Perform AI profiling or automated behavioral analysis
  • Collect data you do not explicitly enter

On-device event logging: We may log certain in-app events (for example, feature usage such as viewing results or starting an export) only on your device. This information is stored locally and is not sent to our servers or to any third party. It is used only to improve the product experience.

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS security features. If you use iCloud sync, your data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Notifications — to send you reminders (e.g. to log sleep or feeding) and to support iCloud sync between your devices.
  • Camera and Photo Library — only to add or save profile photos for your child's profile.

You can change these in your device Settings at any time.

Your Rights

You can delete your data at any time by using the app's "Reset App Data" feature (Settings → Data & Privacy) or by deleting the app. If you use iCloud, you can stop sync by signing out of iCloud in your device settings.

Subscriptions and Payments

All subscription payments are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not receive or store payment information.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
