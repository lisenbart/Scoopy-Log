Simple feeding records in one place

Instead of scraps of paper or scattered notes, keep one log: nursing, bottle, and solid food in the same timeline. Add an entry in seconds so you can stay in the moment. No complicated forms — just tap and log.

Clear daily overview for busy parents

Common questions

Can I track breastfeeding and bottles?

Yes. You can log nursing, bottle, and solid food in the same daily log. One tap per entry so you can record even during a feed.

Is this a feeding schedule or advice app?

No. Scoopy Log is a log only. It does not tell you when or how much to feed. You record what happens; the app helps you see it clearly.

Can I see the day at a glance?

Yes. The app shows today’s entries and a weekly overview so you can review patterns without stress.

Can I export my feeding data?

Yes. You can export a summary PDF (Free) or the full Captain’s Log PDF (Premium) for your own records or to share when needed.

Is my data private?

Yes. No ads, no tracking, no data selling. Your logs stay on your device or in your optional iCloud account. We don’t have access.
