Questions, answered calmly

Is Scoopy Log medical advice?

No. It is a simple logging tool for your own records. It does not diagnose, treat, or recommend.

Do you track me or sell data?

No. Scoopy Log does not use ads, analytics, or third-party tracking. Your data stays on your device or in your iCloud.

How does iCloud sync work?

Sync is optional and uses your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not have access to your data. You can turn it off anytime in system Settings.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free includes basic logging (sleep, feeding, diapers, growth) and a summary PDF export. Premium adds the full Captain's Log PDF with detailed statistics, multiple child profiles, and long-term trends.

Can I track more than one child?

Yes. You can create multiple profiles and switch between them. Premium unlocks full PDF export and stats for each profile.

Can I cancel Premium?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed in your Apple ID settings. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of the billing period.

What devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad. Data can sync across your devices with the same Apple ID when iCloud sync is enabled.
