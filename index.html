What is Scoopy Log?

Scoopy Log is a simple daily tracker designed for parents of babies and toddlers. It helps you keep a gentle record of your child's routines—sleep, feeding, diapers, and growth—so you can stay organized, share updates with caregivers, and have clarity during pediatric visits. Scoopy Log does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. No clutter, no stress.

How it helps parents

Sleep

Track nap times and overnight sleep, with a live Lock Screen timer and quick sleep entry.

Feeding

Log nursing sessions, bottles, and solid foods with simple taps.

Diapers

Keep a record of diaper changes to stay organized and informed about daily care routines.

Growth

Monitor milestones, weight, and height as your child grows.
